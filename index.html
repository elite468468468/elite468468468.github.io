Navid Abapour

About Me: I have been a PhD candidate at the University of Surrey since 2023, where my journey in Trust in Lattice-based Electronic Voting is being pursued under the supervision of Catalin Dragan and Ioana Boureanu at the Surrey Centre for Cyber Security, along with pursuing a PGCert in Teaching & Learning in Higher Education mentored by Karen Gravett at the Surrey Institute of Education. Previously, my research collaboration was at the University of Melbourne in the field of Quantitative Information Flow and earned an Honours BSc in Computer Science with Reza Hooshmandasl from the University of Mohaghegh Ardabili.

Research Interests: Post-quantum Cryptography, Secure Multi-party Computation, Privacy-preserving Computing

Professional Activities:

  • Reviewer of IEEE S&P 2025 (ext.), MDPI Sensors, Arabian Journal of Science and Engineering, Cluster Computing, SN Computer Science, ACM CSAE 2023
  • Member of Institute of Mathematics and its Applications, IEEE: Information Theory Society, ACM: Security, Audit and Control Group, International Association for Cryptologic Research, Computability in Europe: Transfinite Computation Group
