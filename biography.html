About Me

My overwhelming interest in the Computational Systems has led me to be an Honours B.Sc. student since 2018 in Computer Sceince under supervision of Prof. M.R. Hooshmandasl, and my accomplishments and activities caused to be the Youngest Teaching Assistant in the Department of Computer Science, as well as Department of Mathematics and Applications (Lecturer in Charge: Prof. Naser Zamani) and First Undergraduate Research Assistant in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (Principal Investigator: Prof. Shahram Jamali) at University of Mohaghegh Ardabili (UMA). I was a Content Creator at Sharif University of Technology's (SUT) Iranian Society of Cryptology (Scientific Secretary: Dr. Taraneh Eghlidos). Before entering UMA, I earned my Diploma in Mathematical Sciences from Ayatollah Malakouti Elite High School in East Azerbaijan.
Apart from academia, as a B.Sc. student I have passed multiple extra courses with a focus on industrial aspects: Network Security Engineering (2021), Huawei Technologies; Innovation Practice of IoT (2021), Shanghai Jiao Tong University; Engineering and Innovation (2021), Nordic Science Centre Association; Advanced Information Security (2021), The Open University; Information Security Architecture (2020), eQual Assurance; Web Development (2019), Oxford Cert.
